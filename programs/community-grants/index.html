Scroll Community Grants Program

Empowering communities worldwide to drive growth and innovation in the Scroll ecosystem

Apply for a Grant

What We Support

We fund projects that build community, drive adoption, and create meaningful impact across the Scroll ecosystem

Events

Large-scale gatherings with 100+ attendees to strengthen networks, exchange knowledge, and promote Scroll's ecosystem products

Meetups

Community gatherings focused on exploring Scroll, connecting builders, and sparking collaboration through hands-on activities.

Hackathons

Competitions for developers to discover Scroll's technology and build innovative solutions leveraging our ecosystem.

Flex

Creative proposals that don't fit traditional categories, including education programs, scholarships, and unique community projects.

Program Details

Grant Range

$200 - $10,000

Duration

3 Months

Deadline

Dec 19, 2025

Approval Time

Within 30 days of application

What We're Looking For

  • Projects that foster community growth through educational and innovative activities
  • Initiatives that encourage adoption of Scroll ecosystem products
  • Events that increase visibility across different regions and contexts
  • Programs that promote geographic diversity and transparency
  • Proposals with clear KPIs and demonstrable impact
  • Teams with proven experience in community management

How it Works

1
Submit Application

Fill out our application form with your project details, milestone budget and KPIs

2
Review & Feedback

Community Council evaluates your proposal within 7 days and provides detailed feedback when necessary

3
Approval & Funding

Approved grants receive payments on milestone basis

4
Execute & Report

Implement your initiatives and submit a final report with metrics and evidence
